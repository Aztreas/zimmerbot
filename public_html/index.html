ZimmerBot

Welcome to the home page of ZimmerBot, a tool created and maintained by DiversaTech at Berkeley since Fall 2017!

The goal of this bot is to help readers save data while browsing through Wikipedia by downloading article sets about different search categories such as popularity, article score, and other filters.
